Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

This Chicken Tikka Masala recipe is a family favorite. It's rich, creamy, and full of flavor. I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, garam masala, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Add chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until soft.
  3. Add marinated chicken (including the yogurt mixture) to the skillet. Cook until chicken is browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve sauce over rice or with naan.
