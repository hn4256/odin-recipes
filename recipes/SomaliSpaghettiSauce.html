Somali Spaghetti Sauce Recipe

This Somali-style spaghetti sauce recipe has my own little tweak to it. We make this once a week in our house (my daughter craves it!). I hope you enjoy this as much as we do!.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until soft.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
