Beef Tacos Recipe

This Beef Tacos recipe is a family favorite. It's flavorful, easy to make, and always a hit. I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup tomato, diced
  • 1 cup cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef and taco seasoning. Mix well.
  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add seasoned beef and cook until browned.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos by adding beef, lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sour cream to each tortilla.
